Bio

I am a third-year doctoral student within the Quantitative Research, Evaluation and Measurement program in the Educational Studies department at Ohio State University. My advisor is Professor Ann O’Connell. I have a background in Information Systems, and in my downtime, I enjoy using my computing skills to develop statistical tools for applied researchers. My interests are causal inference, communicating statistical findings, program evaluation, statistics education, API development.
